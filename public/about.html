關於 Markdown Live Previewer

Markdown Live Previewer 是一款專為追求效率與隱私的創作者打造的線上編輯工具。

我們的使命

我們致力於提供一個簡單、強大且完全在本地運行的編輯環境。您不需要登入，也不需要擔心您的機密文件被上傳到任何雲端空間。所有的創作過程都發生在您的瀏覽器中。

核心特色

  • 即時預覽：毫秒級的同步滾動與渲染。
  • 專業支援：完美呈現 Mermaid 圖表、LaTeX 公式與 Vega 數據可視化。
  • 隱私優先：100% 本地存儲，無後端伺服器紀錄。
  • 多格式導出：支援高品質 PDF、PNG、SVG 導出。

About Markdown Live Previewer

Markdown Live Previewer is an online editing tool built for creators who prioritize efficiency and privacy.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing a simple, powerful, and entirely local editing environment. No login required, and no need to worry about your confidential documents being uploaded to any cloud. Everything happens right in your browser.

Key Features

  • Live Preview: Millisecond-level synchronization and rendering.
  • Professional Support: Perfect rendering for Mermaid diagrams, LaTeX formulas, and Vega visualizations.
  • Privacy First: 100% local storage with no server-side logging.
  • Multi-format Export: Support for high-quality PDF, PNG, and SVG exports.
